VHX plugin for XBMC
===================

Lets you get a list of videos from your VHX.tv account, which can parse 
videos from various sources:

* Facebook
* Twitter
* Tumblr
* Vimeo (not working yet)
* Reddit
* Boing Boing
* Laughing Squid
* Devour
* Buzzfeed
* VHX staff and community picks
* Movie trailers 

Get a free account at [VHX.tv](http://vhx.tv/)
